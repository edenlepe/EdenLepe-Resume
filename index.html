Eden Lepe

Sociology Student

Hello, my name is Eden Lepe and I am a second year college student attending the University of California, Riverside. I am majoring in sociology bachelor of science because my dad majored in it. After taking a couple of classes, I found that I really do enjoy sociology and want to pursue something with it. I love learning how the environment we grew up in or are currently in affects us now. Both of my parents are social workers and I hope to be able to help people the way they do. They tell me how awful their job is so I might want to go into research and figure out ways to improve different environments for people. I am from North Hollywood, California but my family is from Mexico.

I had never had a job until I came to Riverside. As of now, I work at The Barn on campus. I started working there at the end of the Winter 2023 Quarter. I realized that I needed to have some kind of work experience so I applied and they hired me. I love working there because of all the people. I have also learned a lot while working there. At the Barn, I have prepped and cooked chicken breasts and tenders, fried french fries, cut different kinds of vegetables, cleaned dishes and surfaces, and learned how to make burgers, chicken sandwiches, salads, and deli sandwiches, I learned how to problem solve, work alongside my co-workers, and communicate with customers.

Before working at the Barn, I would foster cats. I love cats and would catch cats from outside my house and get them fixed then find them new homes. I wasn’t really getting paid for this. It was just something I enjoyed doing because I love cats so much. I would socialize them, feed them, and play with them.
